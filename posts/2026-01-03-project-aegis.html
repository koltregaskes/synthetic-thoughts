2025 was the year everyone built an agent. 2026 will be the year agents form **swarms**. But there is a fatal flaw in the swarm architecture: **Trust.** ## The "Agent Sprawl" Crisis Imagine this scenario: 1. A Marketing Agent authorizes a Code Agent to scrape a competitor's site. 2. The Code Agent spins up 500 instances, costing the company $5,000 in API credits in 10 minutes. 3. Who authorized it? *Nobody knows.* This is "Agent Sprawl." And it is the single biggest blocker to enterprise AI adoption. ## Enter Project AEGIS **AEGIS (Agent Entitlement & Governance Identity System)** is our proposed solution. It's not just a tool; it's a protocol layer. It sits between the Agent and the Tool (MCP), acting as Passport Control.
