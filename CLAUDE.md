## Post Format

Posts are markdown with frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: "Post Title"
date: 2026-01-11
tags: [ai, technology]
summary: "Brief description"
---

Post content here...
```

## Writing Guidelines

### Voice
- Kol's authentic style (see `Writing/My Writing Style.md`)
- UK English
- Sceptical but curious
- Evidence-based with opinions

### Structure
- Strong opening hook
- Clear sections
- Practical takeaways
- No hashtags or corporate speak

### Quality Checks
- [ ] No AI vocabulary (delve, showcase, leverage)
- [ ] UK spelling
- [ ] Grounded, not flowery
- [ ] Would Kol actually write this?
